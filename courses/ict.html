What's ICT?

The ICT strand focuses on computer programming, networking, and cybersecurity. Students will study software and hardware design and development, gaining practical experience through internships and projects. This strand prepares students for careers in software development, cybersecurity, network administration, and digital marketing.

Learnings

Here are some things you can learn about Information and Communication Technologies (ICT):

Learn computer programming, networking, and cybersecurity
Study software and hardware design and development
Gain practical experience through internships and projects
Prepare for careers in software development, cybersecurity, network administration, and digital marketing

Career

Job Opportunities

Software Developer

Develop software applications, design and implement coding, and test software to ensure functionality and quality. Collaborate with team members and stakeholders to create innovative software solutions.

Cybersecurity Analyst

Protect computer systems and networks from cyber attacks, monitor security systems and respond to threats. Analyze data to identify vulnerabilities and develop strategies to prevent attacks.

Network Admin

Manage computer networks, configure and install software and hardware, and troubleshoot issues. Ensure network performance and security, and manage user access and permissions.

Digital Marketer

Create and execute digital marketing campaigns, develop social media strategies, and analyze data to improve marketing performance. Utilize a range of digital channels and tools to reach and engage target audiences.
