What's EIM?

The Electrical Installation and Maintenance (EIM) strand in Senior High School prepares students for careers in the electrical industry. They learn about electrical safety, wiring methods, and electrical devices, and receive hands-on training through laboratory activities and industry immersion programs. Graduates can pursue careers as electricians, electrical technicians, or electrical engineers.

Learnings

Here are some things you can learn about Electrical Installation and Maintenance (EIM):

Electrical safety practices
Different wiring methods
Troubleshooting of electrical systems
Hands-on skills through laboratory activities

Career

Job Opportunities

Industrial Electrician

Install and maintain electrical systems in industrial facilities, such as factories and power plants.

Electrician

Install and maintain electrical systems in homes, businesses, and industrial settings.

Electrical Engineer

Design and develop electrical systems for buildings, products, and infrastructure.

Electrical Technician

Troubleshoot and repair electrical systems, machinery, and equipment.
