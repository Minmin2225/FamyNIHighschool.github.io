What's ABM?

The ABM strand focuses on accounting, business, and management principles. Students will study financial management, business ethics, marketing, and other related subjects. This strand provides practical experience through internships and prepares students for careers in finance, accounting, management, and entrepreneurship.

Learnings

Here are some things you can learn about Accountancy, Business and Management (ABM):

Learn accounting, business, and management principles
Study financial management, business ethics, and marketing
Gain practical experience through internships
Prepare for careers in finance, accounting, management, and entrepreneurship

Career

Job Opportunities

Accountant

Manage and analyze financial records, ensure compliance with tax laws and regulations, and provide financial advice and guidance to clients. Play a key role in helping organizations make informed financial decisions.

Financial Analyst

Analyze financial data and trends, create financial models and forecasts, and provide insights and recommendations to clients or organizations. Help businesses and investors make informed investment decisions.

Marketing Specialist

Develop and execute marketing strategies and campaigns, conduct market research and analysis, and build and maintain relationships with customers and stakeholders. Help organizations promote their products or services and achieve their business goals.

Entrepreneur

Start and manage a business venture, identify and seize new opportunities, and create innovative solutions to meet market needs. Build and lead a team, and take on the risks and rewards of being a business owner.
