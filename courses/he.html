What's HE?

The HE strand focuses on the study of food science and nutrition, textile and fashion, and home management. Students will study interior design and home decoration, gaining practical experience through cooking and sewing classes. This strand prepares students for careers in culinary arts, fashion and textile design, interior design, and hospitality management.

Learnings

Here are some things you can learn about Home Economics (HE):

Study interior design and home decoration
Food science and nutrition, textile and fashion
Gain practical experience through cooking and sewing classes
Prepare for careers in culinary arts, fashion and textile design, interior design, and hospitality management

Career

Job Opportunities

Chef

Prepare and cook food in restaurants, hotels, or other food service establishments. Plan menus, create recipes, and manage kitchen staff.

Fashion Designer

Design and create clothing and accessories, develop new fashion ideas, and stay up-to-date with the latest fashion trends.

Intertior Designer

Design and plan the layout and decoration of interior spaces, such as homes, offices, or public spaces. Choose materials, colors, and furniture to create a functional and aesthetically pleasing environment.

Hospitality Manager

Manage hotels, resorts, and other hospitality establishments. Oversee daily operations, such as staffing, guest services, and financial management, to ensure the success of the business.
